Teaching Method


We teach to undertand and judge the gist, assure clarity of concept; teach to deduce relationship from facts and figure; develop ability to reason; develop ability to engage in abstract thinking; teach students to learn and understand new material and profit from past experience.

Test & Examination System


After all classes and syllabus coverage. We introduce test system.
Firstly after every month we conduct test of all students and than we annouce their results by sending sms to thier guardian/parents.
Secondly, After completion of course we conduct three hour of exam like Board exam. In this we cover all topics so students are well aware about their final board exam.
This methodology prepares students to appear in exam with well preparation. By this our students secured great marks in their final examination.
